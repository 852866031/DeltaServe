Key exp6 findings:
  - All real content: mean_imbalance = 0.982–1.037 (perfectly balanced)
  - Random tokens: mean_imb ~1.086, max_imb ~1.74 (MORE imbalanced than real text)
  - lag1_autocorr across layers: mean = -0.13 to +0.03 (mean-reverting, not correlated)
  - frac_r0_heavy: mean ~0.47, always in [0.33, 0.63] — symmetric across ranks
  - corr(imbalance, actual_ms): near zero for all groups
  CONCLUSION: Routing is balanced. The ~5% CV is irreducible hardware/comms variance.
